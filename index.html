Utah

Utah is a state in the western United States. It is bordered by Colorado to the east, Wyoming to the northeast, Idaho to the north, Arizona to the South, and Nevada to the west. It also touches a corner os New Mexico in the southeast. The state is a center of transportation, education, information technology, and research, and a major tourist destinationfor outdoor recreation. Salt Lake City, the state capital and largest city, is home to the headqueaters of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (LDS Church).
