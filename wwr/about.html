Willamette River Rafting

Here at Willamette River Rafting, we prioritize safety while also giving you the best rafting experience you can get. We hope you can trust us to make your rafting experience the best one yet. We have over 20 years of experience making sure people are safe and having fun while river rafting. Our employees are thoroughly trained to ensure safety, as well as being able to guide you through your rafting journey.

History

Our company was founded in 2006 when our founder saw that there weren’t that many rafting companies along the Willamette River, and those who were around didn’t fit his standard for safety or fun, so he opened his own company, wanting to ensure that there was a Rafting company that was not only safe, but fun for anyone who joins, from beginners to pros.
